## 🚀 Overview
Red Giant Magic Bullet for Windows is a suite of color-grading and finishing plugins built for filmmakers and video editors who want their footage to look like it came off a cinema screen. At its heart sits Magic Bullet Looks, the flagship tool that pairs a visual grading interface with a library of ready-made presets, so you can move from flat camera footage to a polished, graded shot in minutes. Because the whole suite runs as plugins, everything happens inside the editors you already use, keeping your grading work in the same timeline as the rest of your project.

## ✨ Features
- **Magic Bullet Looks** — Grade shots on a visual set with cinematic presets, then fine-tune color, contrast, and lighting with intuitive on-screen controls.
- **Full plugin suite** — The Red Giant Magic Bullet suite bundles several finishing plugins, from film-look emulation to denoising and cosmetic skin smoothing, in one package.
- **Cinematic presets** — Start from professionally designed looks and adapt them to your footage instead of building every grade from scratch.
- **Editor integration** — Apply Magic Bullet as an effect layer inside Premiere Pro and After Effects, keeping grading non-destructive and reversible.
- **Skin and film finishing** — Smooth skin tones, add authentic film-style grain and character, and clean up noise for a finished, professional result.

## 🎯 Getting Started
1. Close Premiere Pro or After Effects and run the Red Giant Magic Bullet installer so it can register the plugins with each host.
2. Reopen your editor and load the clip you want to grade onto the timeline.
3. Apply the Magic Bullet Looks effect to the clip the same way you would any other video effect.
4. Open the Looks interface, pick a cinematic preset as a starting point, and refine the color, contrast, and lighting tools until the grade fits your shot.
5. Add other suite plugins, such as film-look or skin-smoothing effects, to finish the shot before you export.
